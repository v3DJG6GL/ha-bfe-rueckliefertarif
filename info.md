# BFE Rückliefertarif

Home-Assistant-Integration für den Schweizer PV-Rückliefertarif gemäss Art. 15 EnFV (ab 1. Januar 2026).

Rewrites long-term statistics of the Energy-Dashboard compensation entity using the official BFE quarterly reference market price plus national Mindestvergütung/Anrechenbarkeitsgrenze rules. Supports 11 Swiss utility presets.

See the [README](https://github.com/v3DJG6GL/ha-bfe-rueckliefertarif) for full documentation.
